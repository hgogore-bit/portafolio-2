ABOUT ME

I’m Hugo Garcia Ortega, an Art Director and Senior Motion Designer with 9+ years of experience creating digital campaigns, visual content and multimedia assets for brands across Mexico and Latin America.

My work combines art direction, motion design, video editing, creative production and AI-powered workflows to turn ideas into strong visual pieces for social media, digital platforms, campaigns and branded content.

I’ve led creative teams, collaborated with marketing, UX/UI and product teams, and developed multimedia content for brands such as Samsung, Bimbo, Quaker State, Roche Group and Televisa.

I’m interested in projects where craft, storytelling, technology and business goals meet.
